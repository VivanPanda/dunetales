About Us

Why DuneTales

Empowering People by Sharing Stories with Respect

DuneTales amplifies the voices of migrant workers in Dubai through recorded conversations and shared stories. We believe storytelling builds empathy, opens opportunities, and inspires meaningful support.

  • Give migrant workers a platform to share their experiences

  • Preserve authentic voices with dignity and care

  • Create understanding through personal narratives

  • Connect listeners with ways to make a difference

We envision a world where every migrant worker's story is heard, valued, and respected. A future where storytelling bridges communities and drives positive change.

  • A global archive of migrant worker experiences

  • Greater empathy and awareness in host communities

  • Stronger support networks for workers far from home

  • Stories that inspire policy and social change

Our process is built on trust, consent, and collaboration. We work directly with migrant communities to capture and share their stories in ways that protect and empower them.

  • Collect stories with informed, clear consent

  • Edit and translate with cultural sensitivity

  • Protect privacy and anonymity when requested

  • Share through audio clips and written highlights
